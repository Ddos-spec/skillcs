# Followme Product Knowledge

## Re-Branding & New Formulation

The **Followme Classic** line has been refreshed with cleaner packaging and updated scents. Each bottle now clearly states the variant name and fragrance type to make identification easier. The scent DNA has also been overhauled:

- **Old DNA:** citrus and floral notes with saffron, jasmine and ambroxan leading into amber and cedar.
- **New DNA:** fresh bergamot, grapefruit and mandarin top notes; rhubarb and geranium in the heart; a woodier dry‑down with Chinese black tea, musk, cedarwood and guaiac wood.

- **L’Âme de l’Océan** – A relaxing yet energizing marine scent. Plum and blackcurrant lead into fig milk and violet leaves; the base features coconut milk, moss and sandalwood. Perfect for holidays, gym sessions and extreme activities.
- **Sunset Flavor** – A magical sunset in a bottle. Nashi pear and kumquat meet caramel and tuberose on a base of ambergris, musk and tonka bean. Suitable for sport, college life, hangouts and casual dates.
- **Senso di Blossom** – A feminine bloom with lime, bergamot and pineapple on top; lily of the valley and jasmine in the heart; and white peach, vanilla and musk in the base. Great for birthdays, parties, weddings and professional settings.
- **Tobacco Noire** – From the Tobacco Series: combines authentic tobacco warmth with clove spice, lily of the valley and cedarwood, finishing with musk, vanilla and tonka beans. Provides a luxurious alternative to smoking; ideal for airplanes, public transport and hangouts.
